# Dollar-Cost Averaging

Dollar-cost averaging means investing a fixed amount on a regular schedule. This approach can reduce the pressure to choose the perfect day to invest.

When prices are lower, the fixed contribution buys more shares. When prices are higher, it buys fewer shares. The approach can support consistent behavior, but it does not guarantee better returns than investing a lump sum.

The best approach depends on cash flow, risk tolerance, and goals.
