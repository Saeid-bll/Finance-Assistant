# Index Funds

An index fund attempts to track a market index, such as a broad stock or bond index. Many index funds are designed to provide diversified exposure at relatively low cost.

Index funds do not try to pick winning securities. Instead, they aim to capture the performance of the selected index, minus expenses and tracking differences.

Investors should still compare fees, holdings, and the risk level of the index being tracked.
