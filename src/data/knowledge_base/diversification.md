# Diversification

Diversification means spreading money across different investments, asset classes, sectors, or regions. The goal is to reduce the harm caused by one holding performing poorly.

A diversified portfolio may include broad stock funds, bond funds, cash, or other assets depending on the investor's time horizon and risk tolerance.

Diversification can reduce concentration risk, but it does not eliminate market risk or guarantee gains.
